Natural Ingredients,
Extraordinary Results.

Could one single ingredient be changing the entire skincare industry? The answer is yes.

In 2010, scientist and cosmetics executive Patti Pao traveled to Western Norway on business. In a thinly-veiled attempt to get out of hiking with clients, she made plans to tour a famous salmon hatchery in the Norwegian countryside. The tour would change her life forever. Inside the fishery, she noticed something peculiar: The workers had astonishingly youthful looking hands. In fact, their hands looked about 20 years YOUNGER than their chronological ages. It turns out, the secret was in the water.

See, this particular facility practiced synchronized hatching, where hundreds thousands of salmon eggs hatch underwater at the same time. And when salmon hatch, they release a special enzyme. This enzyme dissolves their shells, creating an opening for the baby salmon to swim out without being harmed.
